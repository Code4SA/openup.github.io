We empower citizens to improve their lives and communities.

We build tools, open up data, and provide training that support active citizenry and help communities and government work together.

Our work

Liberate
We make data available and easily accessible so journalists, governments, and everyday people can better understand the world around them
The biggest collection of freely available gazettes in South Africa
Educate
Through our short courses we teach young and old about data; understanding, sourcing, cleaning, analysing, and visualising it
By-laws for Cape Town, JHB and Durban - online and searchable
Cooperate
We work with governments to make their data freely available so the people they serve can more easily hold them accountable
How is your municipality managing your money?
Empower
Through our tools, training, and other work, we want to empower people to improve their lives and their communities
What should your medicines cost?
